Passion

One can never consent to crawl when one feels the impulse to soar - Helen Keller

I like to get the most out of everything. Not necessarily the most attention or power or prestige... just the best part of something... anything. In my marrow there is an impulse for flight. I guess that's why music has been so instrumental in my life. It really gives me wings... to ascend negativity and hardship. I enjoy sharing that with the world. Its magic. Software is magic. It can take a dreaded task completely out of your life. It can give you time. The most valuable resource of all. I enjoy spending my time making helpful things. I like to see joy in the faces of the people around me. I love to see the gears turn whenever someone shares their new idea. I savor the sensation of a new idea that occurs to me. My life is for making the world a better place. The progression of technology does this one tiny app at a time. I'm happy to be a part of it. I would love to be a considered for a part in your idea.
