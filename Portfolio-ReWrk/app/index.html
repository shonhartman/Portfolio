history

By nature, I'm a scatter brained day dreamer... right brain all the way. As technology has improved, I started stringing together various apps to 'supplement' my brain's left hemisphere allowing me to become incredibly organized and balanced. Arriving at this balance has given me great joy. I have spent the past 10 years sharing this joy with my community. Now I move to make my own mark on software design.

Examples of Work

here are some of my favorite projects

Easy Recipe App

This was my final project at The Iron Yard. I dreamed of this app while frustrated in the kitchen, squinting at the tiny details of complex recipes. This app is designed to simplify workflow in the kitchen. I used HTML5, CSS3, SASS, Bourbon, and Angularjs to make it.

Field Guide to Detroit

This is a site that I designed off of a wonderful series of tour guides by Wildsam Field Guides. It was the first static website we did in class using HTML5 and CSS3

Powell-Peralta

This is a fun site that we remade at The Iron Yard. I love to skate. So, this was a treat. It was our first exercise in responsive design. I really got comfortable using SASS on this project.
